Consent Privacy Notice

ISEYOU Amazon Ads Analytics

This Privacy Notice explains how 佛山市奕光信息科技有限公司 accesses and processes Amazon Ads information when authorized through Login with Amazon and Amazon Ads API permissions.

Application ISEYOU Amazon Ads Analytics
Operator 佛山市奕光信息科技有限公司
Region China
This application is currently intended for internal company use as a read-only Amazon Ads analytics and optimization tool. It is not offered as a public consumer service.

1. Application Overview

ISEYOU Amazon Ads Analytics helps authorized internal users review Amazon Ads performance, identify optimization opportunities, and support advertising decision-making. The application primarily performs read-only analysis of advertising data made available through Amazon Ads APIs after OAuth authorization.

2. Information Accessed

The application may access Amazon Ads information associated with authorized advertising accounts, including:

  • Campaign, ad group, keyword, target, search term, and placement data.
  • Performance metrics such as spend, clicks, orders, and sales.
  • Advertising profile information needed to identify authorized profiles and accounts.
  • OAuth tokens required to authenticate authorized API requests.

The application does not collect, request, or store Amazon account passwords.

3. Purpose of Processing

The accessed information is processed only for internal business purposes, including:

  • Generating advertising performance reports and dashboards.
  • Analyzing campaign efficiency, keyword performance, targeting results, and placement trends.
  • Supporting advertising budget review, optimization decisions, and account operations.
  • Maintaining reliable, auditable access to authorized Amazon Ads data.

4. OAuth Authorization

Access to Amazon Ads data occurs only after an authorized user grants permission through Login with Amazon and the applicable Amazon Ads authorization flow. Authorization may be revoked according to Amazon's available account and application access controls.

OAuth tokens and the application Client Secret are not written into this webpage or any public client-side code.

5. Storage and Security

Authorized data and OAuth credentials are handled using internal access controls designed to limit availability to personnel and systems with a legitimate business need. Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect stored information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or loss.

This static privacy notice uses only HTML and CSS. It does not operate a server, database, tracking script, or data collection form.

6. Data Sharing

Amazon Ads data accessed by the application is used internally by 佛山市奕光信息科技有限公司. We do not sell advertising data. Data is not shared with third parties except where necessary for internal operations, legal compliance, security, or service providers acting under appropriate confidentiality and data protection obligations.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Advertising data and OAuth tokens are retained only for as long as needed for the internal analytics, reporting, security, compliance, and operational purposes described in this notice. When data or tokens are no longer needed, they will be deleted or de-identified according to internal retention practices and applicable requirements.

Requests about deletion or access revocation may be sent to 904150681@qq.com.

8. International Processing

The application is operated by 佛山市奕光信息科技有限公司 in China. Depending on internal systems, authorized users, infrastructure, and service providers, information may be processed in countries or regions other than where the advertising account is located, subject to appropriate safeguards and applicable law.

9. Policy Updates

This notice may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the application, internal practices, Amazon Ads API requirements, or legal obligations. Updated versions will be made available at this same Consent Privacy Notice URL or another designated URL.
